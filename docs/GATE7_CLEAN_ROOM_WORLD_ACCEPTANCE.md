# Gate 7 World acceptance: neutral clean-room cartridge

**Status:** candidate acceptance contract for the RODOH 1.0 generalization proof.

**Dependency:** exact accepted Arc Gate 7A merge `94c0e965c09e090d768e73ad29f7820aa50606c0` and its unbundled cartridge **The Orchard at Low Tide**.

## Purpose

Gate 7 establishes that Rodoh is a cartridge player rather than a five-cartridge bespoke application. The proof cartridge was authored after all five first-party references through the ordinary generic Arc contract. World must receive it without adding an engine rule, source-plane registration, receiver branch, role vocabulary, theme, trust exception, or Program number.

The proof is intentionally visually neutral. Adding Orchard-specific production art would weaken the acceptance claim because it would avoid testing the holder-owned fallback that every unaffiliated cartridge requires.

## Neutral receiver contract

After ordinary file import, the cartridge must:

- appear as holder-owned and `imported-unsigned`;
- display its exact computed digest;
- remain outside `PROGRAMS_OF_RECORD`;
- receive no `data-cartridge` first-party palette scope;
- resolve to the neutral Rodoh theme and motif fallback;
- borrow no First Charter, Waking Tower, Ilyon, Lamp District, or Relief Circuit environment, portrait, body, motif, emblem, or fiction;
- render its own unfamiliar names directly from the Arc.

The unfamiliar vocabulary includes Graftwright, Tide Diver, Lantern Clerk, Rain Custodian, Civic Credit, Rootstock, Lanterns, Season Standing, and the five civic-orchard challenges. Rodoh may lay out and localize its own controls. It may not normalize those authored names into first-party concepts.

The accepted desktop and mobile journey is:

1. Cold-boot the local cartridge bay.
2. Import the raw generic Arc through the real file input.
3. Inspect holder-owned trust, exact digest, and absence of Program status.
4. Enter through the ordinary transition and make the authored opening choice.
5. Inspect Board, Map, Hall, Aperture, World, roster, resource, and state presentations.
6. Complete Count the Brackish Wells.
7. Complete Negotiate the Graft Exchange with a visibly different resource-spend decision.
8. Complete Dive the Moon Cistern.
9. Complete Replant the Ninth Orchard.
10. Complete Publish the Next Season.
11. Export the changed `axm-cartridge-run/v3` record.
12. Clear the holder context.
13. Import the exact exported run.

Roster choice is also exercised through visible encounter controls. The test selects different lawful squads rather than accepting a hidden scripted party.

## Failure conditions

Gate 7 fails if the candidate introduces:

- an Orchard cartridge id branch;
- a new registered source plane;
- a receiver-specific schema or resolver;
- a hardcoded Orchard role or resource mapping;
- an Orchard theme or first-party asset binding;
- a self-authored trust upgrade;
- loss or interpretation of unknown namespaced memory;
- a release receipt assembled from different candidate heads.

The control question is whether Rodoh can host a world it did not anticipate while keeping authorship, execution, and holder memory in their proper custody domains.
